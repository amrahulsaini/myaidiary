⚙️ Settings

⚠️ Active mode replies to every message in groups Zoop is in — highest ban risk by far. Use only where it's welcome, and switch off at the first WhatsApp warning.

🎙️ Voice notes

Zoop can reply with a human-sounding AI voice note instead of text — in both DMs and groups. It always voices long replies, plus a random share of shorter ones. Each voice note costs a little Gemini usage (~1–2¢) and takes a few seconds longer. ⚠️ In active groups this can add up fast and raises ban risk — keep the chance modest there.

⚠️ Danger zone

Deleting your account unlinks WhatsApp and permanently wipes all your data. This cannot be undone.

WhatsApp → Settings → Linked devices → Link a device → scan.

Use “relink” if messages show in WhatsApp but not here — it resets a corrupted session. Your chats stay; you just scan a new QR.

📥 Import contacts

Export your contacts from your phone as a vCard (.vcf) — e.g. Contacts app → Export, or Google Contacts → Export → vCard — then upload the file or paste it below. You can also paste plain lines like Aditi, +91 86196 03117.
